Ambartrade USA is an international frozen fish broker service company located in Bal Harbour-Miami operating directly with Patagonian sea products.

We are an integrated business that provides insight to trends and ensuring that our clients receive high quality products at the most competitive prices.

Our goal is to be a unique an recognized player in the quality fish market

Our strategy to reach this goal includes:

  • Selecting the most qualified suppliers for each operation.
  • Operating efficiently and responsibly while maintaining fair trade practices.
  • Providing de possibility to develop new products to our customers, allowing the to achieve tangible results.
